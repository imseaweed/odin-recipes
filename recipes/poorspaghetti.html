Poor Man's Spaghetti Recipe

How to make Spaghett

This recipe comes from the depths of my childhood. This is a recipe made from laziness and lack of normal ingredients. It is not very good but it gets the job done.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pan
  • Ketchup
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add Noodle
  3. Drain water (IMPORTANT STEP!)
  4. Place noodle in bowl
  5. Add Ketchup
  6. Eat
